Now Hire with Ease

YourEmployeeCheck is an initiative to ease out some of the challenges faced by Employers while hiring a new candidate. It's an online database where Employers can post their job offers & inform other employers which candidate has been hired by which employer.

About Product

There are many problems in hiring such as:

An employer gives a job offer letter to an employee. But that employee gets a few more offers before joining the company; & sometimes the employee switches to the other company’s job offer, In such cases, the first company suffers in terms of :-

  1. Recruitment Time spent,
  2. Hiring costs occured, etc.
  3. Also Unreliabilty, The employer has to start process again & wait another 1-3 months for a new candidate; & the new candidate also might not join.

YourEmployeeCheck is an online database where employers can update their job offers and give intimation to all other companies that this candidate is already hired by this employer. Also, the employer can enable real-time tracking of the job offer of a candidate as a result if their candidate is getting any other job offer from another employer, the previous employer would get an intimation.

Also, an employer can search on the portal whether the new candidate they are interviewing has already any other job offer or not. As a result,


Features

  1. Search job offers of an candidate: Employers can search the number of job offers a new candidate already has before even interviewing.
  2. Real Time intimations of job offers refusal: Employer can enable Real time intimations of job offers rejection by the new candidate. In case of multiple job offers by many employer to an candidate, a candidate can accept only one offer letter at any point of time. Hence employers can be ready with the backups.
  3. Real Time intimations of other job offers: Real Time intimations of other job offers: Employer can get Real time intimations of other job offers of a new candidate. In case of multiple offers, a candidate can accept only one offer letter at any point of time 7 has to reject other offers.
  4. Moonlighting Detection: Employers can update their employees information on our portal, & Employees working simultaneously on another employer can be tracked.
  5. Reliability: A fast, better & reliable solution to track candidate’s availability.
